WHAT ARE WE TEACHING?

Uni-Safe explores various aspects of university health and safety, teaching important skills, tips, and tricks!
Our modules cover interactive online CPR training, the do's and dont's surrounding alcohol consumption in a university setting, and how to use UWA's resources to prioritise your health and safety in order to gain the uni-experience of your dreams.

ABOUT US

We are a team of UWA students each in different stages of our individual degree plans. Between the four of us, we have learnt many lessons over our time at UWA, which includes an accumulative 7+ years of living on campus.
When it comes to campus life, and the importance staying safe and healthy has on one's overall uni success, we have a lot to say.
Our purpose is to use our own experiences to educate other UWA students on the actions you can take to increase your safety. We educate with the aim to encourage students to make this a priority and reduce the risk of future mishaps that could potentially be fatal for your studies, or even...life.
